**To Make Corrections:**

- Go to the [2024 Tag Set](https://archiveofourown.org/tag_sets/18841).
- Click the _My Nominations_ button on the top right of the page under the menu
  bar.
- Click the _Edit_ button on the top right of the page under the menu bar.
- Click the **X** to the right of the tag, and reenter the corrected tag in the
  text box that appears.
- Click the _Submit_ button at the bottom of the page.
